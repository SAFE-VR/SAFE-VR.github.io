## Why SAFE-VR

Social virtual reality allows people to meet, collaborate, and socialize in
immersive digital environments. However, these spaces can also expose users to
harassment or unsafe interactions. Because VR creates a strong sense of
presence, negative behaviors in virtual spaces can have real psychological
effects on users.

SAFE-VR explores how technology can help create safer and more inclusive
virtual environments. The project develops tools that can detect harmful
behaviors in social VR and provide users with better ways to protect
themselves.

## Research Directions

  <section class="about-pill-card">
